Here’s something about myself

Everything about design ignites a spark in me to visualize my own experiences, moods and understanding of this world, to ensemble a unique form of art.

I am a Creative Designer from Delhi, India. I did my Bachelor’s in Fine Arts (Visual Communication) from Amity University, India.

I worked in Byju’s- India’s largest Ed-tech company, as a Senior Creative Associate. I have also worked as a freelance Graphic Designer which helped me polish my skills and make new networks.

Currently I am pursuing Interactive Media Management from Algonquin College, Ottawa, Canada.
